About TVESD

The Visual Encyclopedia of Skin Disease (TVESD) is an educational initiative focused on improving diagnostic awareness across diverse skin tones. It exists to address long-standing gaps in dermatologic imagery and medical education.

Founder

Keisha Joy

Founder of TVESD with a focus on health equity, public health, and accessible medical education. This project was created to support inclusive learning for students, clinicians, and healthcare professionals.
